Balut Rules

The basic rules are the same as 5-dice Yahtzee except that there are totally 7 scoring categories and each category can be chosen 4 times, and scoring rules are somewhat different.

Scoring

Category Combination Score
4 Dice with number four Total of fours
5 Dice with number five Total of fives
6 Dice with number six Total of sixes
Straight Five sequential dice
(1,2,3,4,5 or 2,3,4,5,6)		 Total of all dice
Full House Three dice of a number and two of another Total of all dice
Choice Any combination Total of all dice
Balut All five dice of the same number 20 plus total of all dice
